Althea is a space held by Aimée Georgiou Lormand - Medical Herbalist, Bodyworker, Community Food Grower, Plant educator and Artist.

Within this site you will find the basic information surrounding these different strands and ways in which you can engage in them. The core values and spirit behind these offerings stem from the belief that as we support the vitality of ourselves through plant connection we can build strong, healing, interconnected foundations which have the potential to ripple out positive change from the individual into their communities and the wider collective.
