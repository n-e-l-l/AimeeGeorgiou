PLANT PHOTOGRAPHY AND ART

As each cycle of the season turns Althea collections releases a limited edition of plant photography prints fresh from Aimée observations of these plant and nature cycles. Each set containing 10 prints named and marked by the Celtic Calendar names. This calender points towards the key changing moment of the season every 6 weeks: Imbolc, spring equinox, Beltane, Summer Solstice, Lammas, Autumn Equinox, Samhain and Winter Solstice mark these moments with beautiful symbology through tradition and folklore. Althea collections aims to capture what these times represent, guided by the plants that express their becoming and peak expressions through their emergence and vitality.

Individual plant art pieces are also presented through watercolour paintings and sold individually as wall hangings or cards for other plant seekers and lovers.
